About Us

Serving unforgettable flavours since 1982.

Our Story

The Spice House began its journey in 1982 with one simple vision — to serve delicious food made with fresh ingredients and authentic recipes. Over the years, we have become a favourite destination for families and food lovers.

Our menu combines traditional North Indian flavours, Chinese favourites, handcrafted pizzas, and popular fast-food dishes, ensuring that everyone finds something they love.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create memorable dining experiences through quality food, excellent service and warm hospitality.

Our Vision

To become the most loved family restaurant by maintaining our tradition of taste and customer satisfaction.

Quality Promise

Fresh ingredients, hygienic kitchen, experienced chefs and exceptional service.
